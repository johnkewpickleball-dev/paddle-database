Firepower & KewCOR

Firepower is an overall 0–100 shot-power rating: Low 0–33, Medium 34–66, High 67–100.

KewCOR measures power at the paddle's sweet spot on a 0.340–0.470 scale: Control 0.340–0.369, All-Court 0.370–0.399, Power 0.400–0.446, Beyond Spec 0.447–0.470.

Core Materials Glossary

Reference data behind the "Inside the Core" table on each paddle. Structural Stiffness is rated 1 (softest) to 10 (firmest).

Note: honeycomb polypropylene used in the paddle handle is never counted as a core material — only the core itself is included anywhere on this page.
